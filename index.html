A PREMIER CARDANO STAKING POOL & COMMUNITY

When Ouroboros Delegation goes live on the Cardano network, every holder of ADA will have the ability to delegate their stake to a pool. In doing so, they play a role in securing the Cardano network and are regularly rewarded with ADA payouts.

Establishing a staking pool is just the first step for Nemean Collective. Eventually we aim to offer a portfolio of tools to our members to enable participation with the Cardano governance system.

STAKING REWARDS

By delegating your ADA holdings to The Nemean Collective you will receive rewards regularly. The amount of reward that you will earn is proportional to the size of your stake compared to the total number of ADA coins delegated to the pool.

MAXIMUM UPTIME

We will be running 24/7 with a high-quality connection to the Cardano network. With high-availability servers and redundancies in place your rate of return for staking will be as high as possible.

NETWORK BETTERMENT

We want to support Cardano in ways beyond staking and will constantly be seeking input from the collective on our roadmap. With planned features like a project proposal & voting system, we want to empower our members to be involved.
